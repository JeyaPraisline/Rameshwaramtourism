Getting there


By Road

Regular Bus services make travelling to the temple town easier and for many, convenient. Places like Nagpur, Hyderabad, Madurai, etc are can act as 'via-points' for those coming from northern part of India- buses, cars or taxis.


By Air


Madurai, at 163 Km distance from the holy town, welcomes the domestic pilgrims where as Chennai the international ones. From where, hired taxis could take to the temple.


By Train


Rameshwaram Railway Station is a five platform railway station and has rail links to major stations such as Coimbatore, Trichy, Madurai, Thanjavur and Chennai.
